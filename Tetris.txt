 Tiny 16-bit Tetris for Boot Diskette ;)

 Sources:

 To compile this you need BP7 and BP7-OS2 by Matthias Withopf.
 crt.pas - replacement for original file from BP7-OS2. It correctly handle
   non-standart videomodes (other, than 80x25).
 os2.pas - expanded OS2_16.PAS from this package
 tetris.pas was founded somethere in net and quickly adopted for OS/2 ;)
